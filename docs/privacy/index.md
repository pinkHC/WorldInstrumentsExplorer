# Instrument Atlas Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 24, 2026

Instrument Atlas does not collect, store, or share personal information.

The app does not require an account, does not use third-party analytics, does not use advertising tracking, and does not transmit personal data to our servers.

Instrument Atlas includes built-in educational content, images, and audio samples that are stored locally in the app.

If you contact us for support, we may receive the information you choose to provide, such as your email address and message content. This information is used only to respond to your request.
